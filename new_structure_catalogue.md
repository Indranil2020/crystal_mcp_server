Below is a “no-stone-unturned” catalogue of EVERY structural motif that is routinely (or should be) reachable from a single, research-grade generator.  

--------------------------------------------------

1. Crystalline bulk – periodic 3-D

--------------------------------------------------

(i) All 230 space-group symmetric cells (primitive / conventional / Niggli reduced)  

(ii) Supercells with integer & rational matrix (S, T, U, V, W, …)  

(iii) Non-diagonal supercells (sheared, triclinic, layer-locked)  

(iv) Commensurate & incommensurate long-period super-lattices (e.g. 5×5×1 on 4×4×1)  

(v) Antiphase boundaries, crystallographic shear planes, twin boundaries (coherent, semi-coherent, incoherent)  

(vi) Polytype stacking (ABC, ABAC, 6H, 15R, …) for SiC, ZnS, Co, …  

(vii) Martensitic / Bain paths (tetragonal ⇄ cubic) with full strain tensor sampling  

(viii) High-entropy alloys (equi & non-equi atomic, special-quasirandom, short-range-order metrics)  

(ix) Magnetic orderings: FM, AFM, FiM, non-collinear, spin-spirals, DMI cycloids, 3-q, 4-q, spin glasses (special quasirandom magnetic)  

(x) Spin-orbit coupled relativistic cells (magnetic space groups, 1651 Shubnikov groups)  

(xi) Defective bulk: vacancies (mono, divacancy, trivacancy, Schottky, Frenkel), interstitials (oct, tet, split, crowdion), antisites, gradient defects (1-D, 2-D, 3-D profiles)  

(xii) Dopant / impurity clouds (Poisson, Gaussian, error-function profiles)  

(xiii) Elastic dipoles, defect loops (Frank, prismatic), dislocation dipoles, stacking-fault tetrahedra  

(xiv) Quantum-condensed phases: charge-density-wave, spin-Peierls, Peierls distorted, breathing Kagome, Jahn-Teller distorted  

(xv) Super-conducting cuprate layer sequences (infinite-layer, 123, 214, Hg-1201, Bi-2212)  

(xvi) Cage-like clathrates (I, II, III, H, HS-I, CS-I, CS-II) with guest placement  

(xvii) Zeolite & AlPO framework topologies (IZA 250+ codes)  

(xviii) Prussian-blue analogues, cyanide perovskites, double perovskites (rock-salt, layered, columnar)  

(xix) Hybrid improper ferroelectrics (layered Ruddlesden–Popper, Dion–Jacobson, Aurivillius)  

(xx) Negative thermal expansion, zero thermal expansion engineered cells  

(xxi) Super-hydride high-pressure phases (LaH10, YH9, …)  

(xxii) Super-hydride high-pressure phases (LaH10, YH9, …)  

(xxiii) Energetic materials: cocrystals, eutectics, plastic-bonded explosives with voids  

--------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------

(ii) 2-D magnetic monolayers (CrI3, CrGeTe3, Fe3GeTe2, 2-D Kagome) with full magnetic anisotropy  

(iii) Janus 2-D (broken inversion, e.g. MoSSe) with out-of-plane dipole  

(iv) X-enes: silicene, germanene, stanene, plumbene, borophene, phosphorene, arsenene, antimonene, bismuthene  

(v) 2-D oxides/hydroxides: MXenes (Ti3C2Tx), hydroxides (NiFe-LDH), perovskite mono-oxides  

(vi) 2-D high-entropy oxides / carbides / nitrides  

(vii) Puckered & buckled phases (1T, 1H, 1T′, Td, 1T‴)  

(viii) Grain boundaries (Σ3, Σ5, Σ7, …) with dislocation cores  

(ix) Nanoribbons (armchair, zig-zag, chiral, bearded, cove-edged) with edge passivation (H, OH, F, Cl, S, Se, NH2, NO2)  

(x) Nanomeshes, nanoflakes, quantum dots with exact edge terminations  

(xi) Porous 2-D (g-C3N4, holey graphene, biphenylene)  

--------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------

(i) Twisted homo- & hetero-bilayers (magic-angle graphene, TBG, TMD bilayers) with commensurate & incommensurate Moiré unit cells (band-preserved, lattice-matched, strain-relaxed)  

(ii) Trilayers, quad-layers, pentalayers with arbitrary twist & translation vectors  

(iii) Graphene/h-BN, graphene/α-MoTe2, h-BN/TMD, TMD/TMD aligned or twisted  

(iv) 3-D bulk graphite-like stacks (ABA, ABC, AAB, ACA, rhombohedral, staggered)  

(v) Layer-locked ferroelectrics (CuInP2S6, In2Se3) with out-of-plane polarization  

(vi) Intercalated bilayers (Li, Na, K, Ca, Fe, Co, Cu, Ag, Au, organic molecules)

(vii) Roughened / stepped surfaces (kinks, ad-terraces, vicinal surfaces)
